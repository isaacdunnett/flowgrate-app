Plan or document your monday.com workflows using mondayflow.

Get Started

Purpose

Some boards on Monday.com can get very complicated with a very large workflow and can have over 30 automations. Creating a board like this requires a lot of thought into it before hand. With mondayflow, create flow diagrams for your boards that are easy to understand.

Advantages

  • creating complex boards are a breeze
  • never forget the workflow of a job
  • easily understand what automations are getting triggered and when
  • track where you are in the process of your workflow
  • easily identify where a mistake has been made
